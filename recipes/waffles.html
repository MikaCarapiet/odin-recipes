Waffles

This waffle recipe is the only one you'll need to make homemade waffles with your waffle iron.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking Spray

  2. Make the waffles: Pour the batter onto the hot, prepared waffle iron. Cook until golden brown and repeat with the remaining batter.
